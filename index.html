About Haukai

At Haukai Restaurant we specialise in bringing you delicious Maōri kai. Based in the beautiful Bay of Islands we want you to be able to immerse yourself in Te Ao Maōri.

Haukai means feast; and at Haukai Restaurant we want you to enjoy our fresh and authentic dishes which are a feast for the eye and the stomach!
